dreams
I grew up with many dreams because growing up, I thought I would be given the chance to do it all. Be a fashion designer, be a marine photographer, a journalist, an artist. Only when I learned about college did I realize that I would have to focus on a specific career.


My foremost, greatest dream is to work in a museum, restoring the great masterpieces to glory. It's a quiet and tedious job, requiring years of training. Not to mention an MFA and--eventually--PhD. I plan on trying. I plan on saving up for an education at New York University and pursuing this career.

Another of my dreams, is to become a children's book writer and illustrator.
