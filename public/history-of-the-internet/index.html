The History of the Internet

In this day and age, it's probably clear just how much of our lives depend on the internet. We shop online, interact online, and we even create online. But when did this all start? How did the internet come to be? On this website we answer a few questions--some that even I have been asking--that were answered in my computer science class.

Below are the discussion points tackled, feel free to skim over the overviews and read on.

    From the last days of World War 2 to the dawn of the Cold War, we take a quick look at the modes of communication used in the mid-1900's and the events that fuelled research and development.
